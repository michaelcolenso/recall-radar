## Design Context

### Users
Vehicle owners looking for safety information. They are likely in a high-intent, potentially anxious state, seeking clear, accurate data about their vehicle's safety status. The job to be done is identifying open recalls and understanding the necessary next steps for repair.

### Brand Personality
Utilitarian, Authoritative, Informative.
Emotional Goal: Informed and Empowered.

### Aesthetic Direction
Industrial Design System. Focused on being a precise, reliable tool. Avoids consumer-grade "polish" in favor of utilitarian clarity and data-centric presentation. Brutalist-adjacent but refined for readability.

### Design Principles
1. **Utility First**: Every element must serve a functional purpose. Decor is noise.
2. **Authoritative Clarity**: Use typography and layout to signal the official, serious nature of safety data.
3. **Data Density**: Present information efficiently without overwhelming the user, using clear groupings.
4. **Empathetic Precision**: Translate complex technical jargon into actionable clarity while maintaining accuracy.
